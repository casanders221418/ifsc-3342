Cookie Lineup

We’re proud to be the oldest, most experienced Girl Scout Cookie baker in the United States. Since 1937, we have empowered and supported the nation’s largest girl-led business, helping provide girls with a program that teaches them essential life skills and creates amazing, year-round experiences powered by cookies.
